ABOUT T7AREK







WHAT IS T7AREK?


#T7arek is a youth-driven initiative, started by a group of active young people aged 16 to 20, aiming to inspire Tunisians to become active members of civil society. Our platform offers detailed advice and resources in order to get involved in Volunteering, Entrepreneurship, Invention and Citizenship (Local democracy and citizen journalism).
